Our Journey

Over the past six weeks, our Future Coders Bootcamp has been a transformative journey for aspiring developers. The program was designed to equip participants with hands-on experience in web development, blending creativity with technical skills. Our participants tackled real-world projects, each building on the last, to hone their abilities in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Weekend projects
  • Week 1: Participants created their personal portfolio websites, laying the foundation for their online presence.
  • Week 2: They developed a blog, practicing content structuring and styling.
  • Week 4: They built a functional calculator and a to-do list, diving into logic and interactivity.
  • Week 5: The challenge was to create a mini fintech app, enabling users to track their daily expenses.
